Hi, I'm Shubham Garg

Welcome to my website! My name is Shubham Garg, and I believe in transforming challenges into opportunities. Through my experiences as a motivational speaker and a dedicated disability activist, I share a wealth of insights drawn from overcoming adversity and embracing resilience.

This platform offers a comprehensive look into my journey—a narrative that blends practical advice with heartfelt stories. I discuss the lessons learned along the way and offer inspiration for those striving for growth and empowerment.

As a visually impaired individual and a proud student at the Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad, I am committed to breaking barriers and promoting inclusion. Join me as I continue to learn, overcome challenges, and make a meaningful impact in the world.
